veggie soup

Anna Bureta
Anna Bureta
Anna Bureta

meal example

A step-by-step recipe.

  1. Peel the carrots.
  2. Wash the tomatoes and the peppers.
  3. Cut the veggies into neat 3cm cubes.
  4. Pour water into a pot.
  5. Boil the water.
  6. Add the carrots and the peppers.
  7. Boil the carrots and the peppers for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the tomatoes.
  9. Boil for 10 minutes.
