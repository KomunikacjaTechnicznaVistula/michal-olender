Choosing your first ergomeach board

A step-by-step guide.
  1. Figure out what are your needs.
  2. Find board suitable to your needs.
  3. Print layouts and try them to see if they are comfortable.
  4. Either make it yourself or order if it is a possibility.
  5. Exercise to get comfortable with new layout.
  6. Enjoy.
